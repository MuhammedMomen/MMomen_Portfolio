FusionPOS AI - Project Overview
FusionPOS AI is a next-generation, multi-platform Point of Sale (POS) system designed to empower small to medium-sized businesses with enterprise-grade features and AI-driven business intelligence.

Core Concept
The system provides a unified retail management experience across Desktop, Web, and Mobile, powered by a high-performance FastAPI backend. It leverages artificial intelligence (FusionAI) to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights, helping managers optimize inventory, forecast demand, and improve customer loyalty.

Key Features
1. Multi-Platform Accessibility
Desktop App (Flet/Python): A robust, cross-platform application for onsite checkout and administration.
Web Frontend (React/Material-UI): A modern, responsive web portal for management and POS operations with full i18n support (English/Arabic).
Mobile Companion (Flet/Mobile): A manager-focused dashboard for real-time sales tracking, stock alerts, and employee monitoring on the go.
2. POS & Retail Operations
Touch-Friendly POS: Streamlined checkout process with support for multiple payment methods (Cash, Card, Digital).
Order Management: Hold/Recall orders, receipt generation, and tax/discount calculations.
Service & Rental: Specialized module for tracking equipment rentals and service history.
3. Smart Inventory Management
Real-time Tracking: Granular stock monitoring across multiple warehouses.
Costing Methods: Supports FIFO and Weighted Average costing for accurate financial reporting.
Stock Alerts: Automatic notifications for low-inventory levels.
Supplier Relations: End-to-end purchase order lifecycle management.
4. FusionAI Integration
Natural Language Queries: Ask questions like "What were my top products this week?" and get instant data.
Business Intelligence: Automated daily summaries and smart inventory recommendations.
Forecasting & Trends: AI-powered sales predictions and trend analysis to stay ahead of market shifts.
Intelligent Upselling: Suggests complementary products based on current cart and customer history.
5. CRM & Loyalty
Customer Profiles: Track purchase history and preferences.
Loyalty Programs: Automate points accumulation and rewards.
Insights: AI-driven customer behavior analysis and birthday reminders.
6. Security & Administration
Role-Based Access Control (RBAC): Fine-grained permissions for Admins, Managers, and Cashiers.
Shift Management: Comprehensive tracking of employee hours and performance.
Bilingual Experience: Built-in support for RTL (Arabic) and LTR (English) layouts.
Technical Stack
Backend: FastAPI, SQLAlchemy, PostgreSQL/SQLite.
Frontends: Flet (Desktop/Mobile), React (Web).
AI Engine: Langchain, FusionAI.
